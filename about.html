About REE Global

About Us

Defining African Luxury & Executive Excellence

Royalty Executive Empire Limited (REEL) is a premium lifestyle and corporate branding company specialising in customisable executive gadgets, luxury electronics, and high-end promotional products.

Headquartered in Lagos with a branch office in Benin City, REEL provides innovative, stylish, and functional technology solutions designed for businesses, professionals, institutions, and luxury consumers across Africa.

At the heart of REEL's identity is a simple conviction: luxury, innovation, and branding excellence must work together to help organisations elevate their corporate identity. Every product that leaves our workshop is designed to combine premium executive gifting with genuine functionality, ensuring that our partners create lasting impressions in every boardroom, conference, and client interaction.

Our commitment is centred on quality, professionalism, creativity, and customer satisfaction, delivering world-class products that distinguish REEL as a trusted name in executive branding and luxury corporate gifting.

👁️

Our Vision

To become Africa's leading premium executive gadget and corporate branding company, delivering innovative luxury technology solutions that elevate businesses, empower brands, and redefine executive gifting standards. In parallel, REEL is building a globally recognised African luxury brand, known for premium customisable gadgets, executive innovation, and world-class corporate branding solutions that create lasting impressions.

🎯

Our Mission

REEL exists to provide premium customisable gadgets, luxury electronics, and executive branding solutions that help businesses enhance their corporate identity through innovation, quality, and exceptional customer service — while delivering world-class executive products and corporate gifting solutions that combine luxury, technology, and professionalism, and building long-term partnerships across Africa and beyond.
